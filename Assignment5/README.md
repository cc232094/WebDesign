I liked how Flexbox made it easier to center boxes and text on the screen using content justification and text alignment without having to use trial and error to alter aspects like margins or padding in the particular way I need. I like how Grid could be used to setup almost an entire page all at once instead of having to apply a format to multiple items. I think they can streamline workflow and make future projects easier as less time will have to be dedicated to both determining how to go about formatting items, as Flexbox and Grid are straightforward in terms of what they will change, and actually formatting them, as they each provide ways of altering multiple items at once rather than requiring individual changes. Of the two, I prefer using Grid because it is much better-suited for changing the positioning of images, something that I find much harder to do than positioning text and often struggled with before utilizing Grid.
