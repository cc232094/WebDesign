About The Book

This book follows former Navy SEAL John Clark as he leads a secret counter-terrorist unit comprised of trained professionals from around the globe. They are known as "Rainbow", confronting and stopping various terrorist attacks across several different nations. Despite the seeming randomness of these incidents, they may connect back to a single, overarching plot that could put the entire world in jeopardy.
