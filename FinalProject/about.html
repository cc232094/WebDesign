About

About The Website Author

This website was created by Chase Comella, a student at the University of Montana, as part of a series of assignments for his Introduction to Web Design class. He has dedicated the site to the Oregon Coast, one of his favorite vacation spots, to showcase it for viewers to see and hopefully encourage others to visit it themselves.
